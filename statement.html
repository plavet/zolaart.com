“Art does not solve problems but makes us aware of their existence. It opens our eyes to see and our brain to imagine.”

Magdalena Abakanowicz

My work explores the relationship between human conditioning and environmental concerns. It is constructed from found objects, recycled materials, with elements of craft and street art. I have use traditional craft, contrasting colour pallets, and juxtaposed motifs and imagery in my work to illustrate social struggle for environmental awareness. The foundation of my work derives from my appreciation for a handmade work and environmentally friendly production.

Magdaleda Abakanowicz’s appreciation for textiles and David Suzuki’s commitment to environmental causes, has taken an impact on development and creation of each piece. Each stitch, surface finish, and print is a visual analysis representing natural pattern, which has been altered resulting in inconsistency and lack of consciousness.
